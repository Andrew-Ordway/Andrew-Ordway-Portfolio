About me

Hi, I'm Andrew Ordway, a Mechanical Engineering student at Cornell University. This portfolio showcases my individual projects, highlighting my work in CAD design, mechanical systems, and prototyping. I'm passionate about turning ideas into functional designs and continuously exploring new tools and techniques in mechanical engineering.

Seattle Skyline Hologram

This project showcases a hologram display system combining CAD modeling and mechanical design. It demonstrates the use of visual illusions and rotating platform displays to highlight Seattle skyscrapers and the Seahawks logo.

Mechanical Orrery Assembly

A gravity-driven desktop orrery mechanism demonstrating planetary motion through a counterbalanced lever system. Highlights mechanical design principles and dynamic motion.

Arduino LED Matrix Pong Game

A minimalist, hardware-based version of Pong, built using an Arduino UNO, joystick module, and 8x8 LED matrix. The game runs entirely on Arduino with interactive visual feedback.
